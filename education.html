Education

Education is the foundation of my journey in the field of Information Technology. As a 2nd-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at Eastern Visayas State University - Ormoc Campus, I’ve been building a strong base of knowledge and nurturing my passion for tech.

Here’s an overview of the programming languages I’ve been working with and what they’re used for:

HTML/CSS

These are the building blocks of the web. HTML structures content, while CSS styles and formats it to make web pages visually appealing and user-friendly. I’m still learning, but I’m getting the hang of creating functional and attractive designs.

Python

Known for its simplicity and versatility, Python is widely used for web development, data analysis, automation, and more. With about a year of experience, I’ve gained confidence in problem-solving and project creation. However, the high-level concepts still challenge me—and I’m excited to keep learning.

JavaScript

JavaScript adds interactivity and dynamic features to websites, such as animations, form validations, and user interactions. I’ve just started exploring this language and have minimal experience, but I’m eager to dive deeper and develop my skills.
